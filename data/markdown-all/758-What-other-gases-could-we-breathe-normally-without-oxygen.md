## What other gases could we breathe normally without oxygen?
    
**Upvotes**: 0 | **Comments**: 0 | **Date**: [3y](https://www.quora.com/What-other-gases-could-we-breathe-normally-without-oxygen/answer/Gary-Meaney)

Nothing. Humans are animals, and all (most, rather) animals have to inhale oxygen in order to oxygenate the cells so that they can make Adenosine Tri-Phosphate (or ATP), which all life relies on.
